about

audioo [aw-dee-oh] is a platform that allows the user to mix and play background sounds to promote focusing, studying, and relaxation. More sounds and a better user experience are planned for the near future.

Currently, audioo is in its alpha stage and only offers one page of sound selections, but that will change soon with my own content and user-submitted content. In the meantime, enjoy, and thank you for listening!

-greg

faq

What type of license do these sounds have?

All sounds on this website are under the CC0 license, which means they reside in the public domain.

What did you use to make audioo?

Audioo was created from scratch with plain-old html, css, and javascript. I used howler.js for the audio engine and flaticon for the icons.

Can I contribute audio of my own?

If you do have audio you want to contribute, please follow the guidelines below. If your audio meets the requirements, you can submit your audio on our discord, which is linked below.

What are the submission guidelines?

  • Audio must be in .wav, .aiff, or .flac format.
  • The audio must be at least CD quality (16 bit, 44.1kHz).
  • No longer than one minute, but preferably shorter in duration.
  • Hosted somewhere like freesound so I can download it.
  • You must agree to use CC0 for its license.

The sound doesn't work on my phone!

Outside of making sure the volume is up, having the phone on silent mode can mute the sound on the website.

I found a bug! Where can I report it?

Drop in on our discord to report one! You can find the link below to join.

Is there an active community for audioo?

While we're brand new, you're more than welcome to help us build one! Join us on discord.
