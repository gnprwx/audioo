about

audioo [aw-dee-oh] is my first project as a novice web developer. The idea is to incorporate my musical background and provide personally-made loopable ambient music whilst also providing nature sounds and white noise like other similar platforms.

Currently, audioo is in its alpha stage and only offers one page of sound selections. But that will change soon. As I add more sound categories, I will be working to improve the user experience and efficiency of the site itself. Thank you for listening!

faq

What does audioo have to offer?

Currently, we have one page of soothing background sounds. However, in the near future, audioo will expand on sounds and music to further promote focusing, studying, and relaxation. Check back once in a while to experience what else we've added!

What type of license do these sounds have?

All sounds on this website are under the CC0 license, which means they reside in the public domain.

What do you use to make audioo?

Audioo is created from scratch with plain-old html, css, javascript. I use howler.js for the audio engine and flaticon for the icons.

Can I contribute audio of my own?

Sure! Although, I'll have to screen it to meet what I think would fit with the website itself first. If you do have audio you want to contribute, please follow the guidelines below. If your audio meets the requirements, you can submit your audio on our discord, which is linked below.

What are the submission guidelines?

  • Audio must be in .wav, .aiff, or .flac format.
  • The audio must be at least CD quality (16 bit, 44.1kHz).
  • No longer than one minute, but preferably shorter in duration.
  • Hosted somewhere so I can retrieve it.
  • You must agree to use CC0 for its license.

The sound doesn't work on my phone!

Outside of making sure the volume is up, having the phone on silent mode can mute the sound on the website.

I found a bug! Where can I report it?

Drop in on our discord to report one! You can find the link below to join.

Is there an active community for audioo?

While we're brand new, you're more than welcome to help us build one! Join us on discord.
