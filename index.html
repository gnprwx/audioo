audioo [aw-dee-oh] is a platform that allows the user to mix and play background sounds to promote focusing, studying, and relaxation. We hope you find audioo useful and enjoy the variety of sounds available.

What did you use to make audioo?

Audioo was created from scratch with plain-old html, css, and javascript. I used howler.js for the audio engine and flaticon for the icons.

The sound doesn't work on my phone!

Outside of making sure the volume is up, having the phone on silent mode can mute the sound on the website.

Can I make an audio request, or just say hi?

Of course. Use the contact form to do so! You can also submit your own audio if you'd like.
