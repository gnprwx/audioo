about

audioo [aw-dee-oh] is a platform that allows the user to mix and play background sounds to promote focusing, studying, and relaxation. More sounds and a better user experience are planned for the near future.

Currently, audioo is in its alpha stage and only offers one page of sound selections, but that will change soon with my own content and user-submitted content. In the meantime, enjoy, and thank you for listening!
