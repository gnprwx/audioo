audi∞ [aw-dee-oh] is my first project as a novice web developer. I wanted to incorporate my knowledge of music into the platform, and going beyond providing nature sounds and white noise like other similar platforms. The idea is to apply the concept of loopable background sounds to my original music, enabling users to "mix" the songs in a simple and straightforward manner.

Currently, audi∞ is in its alpha stage and only offers a limited selection of sounds for users to choose from. While I continue to learn front-end development, I will be working on adding more of my own music and sounds to the site while simultaneously improving the user experience.

audi∞ is created from scratch with plain-old html, css, and javascript. It utilizes howler.js as the audio engine.
