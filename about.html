audi∞ [aw-dee-oh] is my first project as a novice web developer. I wanted to incorporate my knowledge of music into the platform, and go beyond providing nature sounds and white noise like other similar platforms. Along with giving myself a project to work on, I wanted audioo to also feature music stems I've personally made, enabling users to "mix" the songs in a simple and straightforward manner.

Currently, audi∞ is in its alpha stage and only offers a limited selection of sounds for users to choose from. While I continue to learn front-end development, I will be working on adding my own music and sounds to the site while simultaneously improving the user experience
